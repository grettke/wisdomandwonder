#+TITLE: How To Disable Global Eldoc Mode

[[https://emacsredux.com/blog/2018/11/13/eldoc-goes-global/][This explains]] how to disable global Eldoc mode

#+name: Remember to require eldoc first
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
(require 'eldoc)
(global-eldoc-mode -1)
#+END_SRC
