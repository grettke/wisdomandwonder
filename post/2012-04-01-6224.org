#+TITLE: Sayre's law

#+BEGIN_QUOTE
  Sayre's law states, in a formulation quoted by Charles Philip Issawi: "In any dispute the intensity of feeling is inversely proportional to the value of the issues at stake."
#+END_QUOTE
