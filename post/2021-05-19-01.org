#+title: What dash should I use when attributing a quotation?

- Use an *EM*-DASH, which is this: =—=
  - /Not/ an *EN*-DASH, which is this: =–=
  - /Nor/ a HYPHEN-MINUS, which is this: =-=
- Do not leave a space between the space and the name.

For example:

#+begin_quote
Wisdom begins in wonder.

—Socrates
#+end_quote
